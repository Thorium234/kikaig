School Life

Beyond academics, students participate in clubs, leadership, sports, and community-oriented activities.

Clubs & Societies

Debate, journalism, science, drama, and environment clubs run weekly.

Sports Teams

Sports include handball, volleyball, athletics, and indoor competitions.

Mentorship

Structured mentorship sessions build confidence, discipline, and leadership.

Field Trips

Educational visits expose students to science, culture, and career pathways.

Community Service

Students participate in outreach and community engagement activities.

Events

Annual school week, talent showcases, and prize-giving events celebrate growth.
