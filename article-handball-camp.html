Handball Team Begins County Preparations

The school handball team has launched a structured preparation program for the upcoming county fixtures. Training sessions focus on fitness, teamwork, and tactical organization.

Coaches noted strong student commitment and encouraged balanced performance in both sports and academics.

Friendly build-up matches are scheduled throughout the month.
