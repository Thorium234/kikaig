About the School

KIKAI Girls High School is committed to nurturing young women through quality teaching, discipline, leadership, and community service.

History

Founded to expand access to quality secondary education for girls, KIKAI Girls High School has grown steadily through strong partnerships with parents, teachers, and the wider community.

The school has built a reputation for balanced development in academics, sports, and character formation.

Mission

To provide holistic education that empowers learners with knowledge, values, and practical life skills.

Vision

To be a leading girls' secondary school producing confident, responsible, and innovative citizens.

Core Values

  • Integrity and responsibility
  • Respect and discipline
  • Teamwork and service
  • Excellence in all areas

Leadership and Governance

The school is managed through a collaborative framework involving administration, departmental heads, teaching staff, and the Board of Management.
