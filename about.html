About KIKAI

History, Values, and Leadership

KIKAI Girls High School has grown through academic discipline, community partnership, and student-centered leadership.

History

The school was established to expand access to quality secondary education for girls in the region and has evolved into a respected center of learning.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide holistic education that empowers girls with knowledge, discipline, and leadership values.

Vision: To be a leading girls' school producing responsible and innovative graduates.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Respect and responsibility
  • Excellence
  • Teamwork and service

Governance

The school is managed through collaboration between administration, faculty heads, the Board of Management, and parents.

Principal's message: "Our goal is to prepare learners for meaningful academic and social contribution."
