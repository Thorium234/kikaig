Academic Awards Ceremony Highlights

KIKAI Girls High School recognized high-performing students during the annual academic awards ceremony. The event celebrated consistency, discipline, and effort across all subjects.

Departmental teachers encouraged learners to maintain focused study routines and peer support groups as the term progresses.

The administration thanked parents and guardians for their continued support in learner development.
