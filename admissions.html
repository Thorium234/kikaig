Admissions

This section provides public admission information for parents and guardians. For official placement and transfer inquiries, contact the school office.

Admission Requirements

  • Copy of admission letter or transfer documentation
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Previous school progress report
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Completed admission form

Joining Instructions (Summary)

  • Report on the official opening day by 8:00 AM
  • Carry labeled personal items as listed in the joining letter
  • Bring required stationery and approved uniforms
  • Attend orientation briefing with parent/guardian

Important Notes

All admission decisions are guided by Ministry of Education regulations and school capacity. Parents are encouraged to use official contacts for updates.
